Care Guides

How Often Should You Water Houseplants?

Most plants prefer slightly moist soil. Stick your finger in the soil—if it's dry 1 inch deep, water it.

Choosing the Right Pot

Always use pots with drainage holes. Your plant’s roots hate sitting in water.

Lighting Basics

Most houseplants love indirect sunlight. South or east-facing windows are great.
